Welcome to MiniScript!

MiniScript is a simple, clear programming language designed to be embedded in other programs, or used as a teaching tool.

DON'T PANIC! MiniScript is very easy to learn. In fact this Quick Reference tells you everything you really need to know about MiniScript in a single page.

If you are a language lawyer or just really love getting into the details, the MiniScript Manual has it all.

But you'll do just fine poking around in the list of topics at left, and experimenting. Don't be afraid to try stuff; you can't hurt anything! Programming is fun, and programming in MiniScript is especially fun.
